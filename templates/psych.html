AP Psychology

Teacher: Mrs. Phillips

The purpose of the year long, advanced placement course in Psychology is to inroduce students to the systematic and scientific study of human behavior and mental processes. Students are exposed to the psychological theories, principles, and phenomena associated with each of the major subfields within psychology. They also learn about the methods psychologists use in science and practice. Scientific topic areas include:

  • Introduction to Psychology and Psychology Careers
  • Biological Bases of Behavior
  • Sensation and Perception
  • States of Conciousness
  • Learning
  • Memory
  • Cognition
  • Personality
  • Thinking, Language, and Intelligence
  • Motivation, Emotion, Social Psychology
  • Research
